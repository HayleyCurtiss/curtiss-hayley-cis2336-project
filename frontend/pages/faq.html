✧ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ✧

1. How do I submit my artwork?

You can submit your artwork through our Submit Art page. Fill out the form, upload your image, and click submit.

2. What types of art can I submit?

We accept traditional art (painting, sculpture, drawing, crafts, mixed media) and digital art (digital painting, photography, GIFs).

3. Is there a submission fee?

No, submitting your artwork is free.

4. How many artworks can I submit?

You can submit as many works as you like with time allowed for reviewing between.

5. How long does it take for my art to appear in the gallery?

Artwork is reviewed and added within 24-48 hours.
