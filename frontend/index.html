Welcome to ArtConnect,

A community platform for beginner and professional artists to discuss, share, sell, and celebrate art.

ArtConnect helps artists showcase their work, promote events, and connect with art enthusiasts everywhere. Join discussion boards, explore our galleries, and sign up for local exhibitions. Beginner or not, ArtConnect is a resource that anyone can enjoy.

Our Top Featured Event:

August 20 at 11:00 AM · 713 Music Hall, Houston TX

HOUSTON'S SUMMER VIBE ART FAIR

Houston’s Summer Vibe Art Fair brings together the city’s most energetic and creative voices for a full day of art, music, and community. Visitors can explore vibrant paintings, handmade crafts, live demonstrations, and interactive workshops while enjoying food trucks and local vendors. The event celebrates Houston’s diverse artistic culture, giving guests a chance to meet emerging creators, discover new styles, and experience the city’s creative heartbeat all in one place. It’s the perfect summer outing for families, students, and anyone looking to soak in some inspiration.

This year’s fair will be held on August 20 at 11:00 AM at 713 Music Hall in Houston, TX, offering plenty of space to browse, mingle, and discover new talent. Guests can find full event details, vendor lists, and workshop schedules at www.summervibeartfair.com, and anyone with questions about tickets or accessibility can reach the organizers directly at @summervibeartfair.com or (713) 555‑0194. It’s the perfect summer outing for families, students, and anyone looking to soak in some inspiration.
