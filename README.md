PROJECT TITLE: ARTCONNECT Website

DESCRIPTION: 

The purpose of ArtConnect is to provide beginner to professional artists a diverse community platform to display and/or sell their art, and a place to discuss their craft. ArtConnect also promotes local art events. Visitors and art enthusiasts are welcome to browse and purchase art. 

KEY FEATURES AND FUNCTIONALITY INCLUDED IN THIS VERSION: 

.Gallery page
.Event page
.FAQ page
.References page
.Art Submission page

.buttons added with javascript
.functional links
.functional art submission previews
.functional calendar for events
.functional youtube video embed on homepage

GOALS: 

.To create an active online community of artists and branch out publicly
.To help artists with visibility and sales
.To provide an easy, welcoming starting point for beginners
.To help those visiting the website or events to interact with art and artists
.To lead art enthusiasts to positive experiences within the app or at events
.To provide a space where artists can advertise commissions 


BENEFITS FOR ARTISTS AND VISITORS: 

Artists: ArtConnect provides visibility for both beginner and professional artists, as well as a chance to connect with potential buyers. It allows them to build an online portfolio. They may also discuss techniques and critiques with fellow artists. The publicity also provides the confidence to enter their art into public events.

Visitors: ArtConnect helps visitors become more familiar with diverse art styles and artists. Visitors can purchase art or just browse. They can engage with and support the local art community.
